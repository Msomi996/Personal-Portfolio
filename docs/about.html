My Career...

Hi!😊 I am a Full Stack Product Designer

I received my Bachelor of Arts in Communication Science at the University of South Africa where I gained valuable skills such as understanding the principles of conducting research, identifying business needs and translating business needs into product requirements.

I went on to become a digital marketer for small businesses but found that I wanted something more creative.

I became fascinated with the way people think, feel and behave - and how digital products could help them live more fulfilling lives. I came across UI/UX design, which is a perfect fit for me.

I completed an internal project management course and a product design bootcamp, and am currently an associate Product Designer at IQbusiness.

Working closely with fellow designers, product managers and participating in usability testing – I have become fascinated with brainstorming and fine-tuning numerous user flows. These are some of the moments that I enjoy the most as a UX/UI designer.

As a designer, I am happy to build innovative features and deliver high quality user experiences. I am very self-motivated, always learning and an optimist who is a problem solver. I love designing interactions that make technology feel effortless.

In my free time, I am often spotted going out with friends, travelling to off-the-grid destinations and probably shopping online.
